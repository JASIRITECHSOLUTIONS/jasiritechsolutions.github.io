About Us

Who we are and what drives us.

Jasiri Tech Solutions is a technology company headquartered in Kenya. We specialise in providing tailored ICT solutions for businesses, organisations, and individuals looking to harness the power of modern technology.


Our name — Jasiri, meaning brave in Swahili — reflects our commitment to tackling complex technical challenges with boldness and creativity. From small businesses getting their first website to enterprises deploying AI-driven systems, we partner with our clients at every stage of their digital journey.


We believe technology should be accessible, affordable, and impactful for every Kenyan business. That's the Jasiri promise.
