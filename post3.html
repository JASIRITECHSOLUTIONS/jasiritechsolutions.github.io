Digital Marketing Essentials

Published by Jasiri Tech Solutions

Digital marketing levels the playing field for Kenyan businesses. With millions of Kenyans online and mobile internet access rapidly growing, a solid digital presence is no longer optional — it's essential.

Your Website is Your Digital Storefront

A professional, fast-loading website builds trust and converts visitors into customers. Ensure it's mobile-friendly, loads in under 3 seconds, and clearly communicates what you offer.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

Most buying journeys start with a Google search. SEO ensures your business appears when potential customers search for your products or services in Nakuru and beyond.

Social Media Marketing

Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn are powerful tools for building brand awareness and engaging your audience. Consistency and authenticity are key.

Email Marketing

Email remains one of the highest-ROI digital channels. Build a list of interested prospects and nurture them with valuable content and targeted offers.

Google Business Profile

Claim and optimise your Google Business Profile so customers can find you on Maps, read reviews, and contact you directly from search results.
