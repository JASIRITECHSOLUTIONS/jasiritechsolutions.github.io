How to Choose the Right ICT Solutions

Published by Jasiri Tech Solutions

Choosing the right ICT solutions is one of the most impactful decisions a business can make. The wrong choice leads to wasted budget, frustrated staff, and lost opportunities — while the right one transforms productivity and growth.

1. Understand Your Business Needs First

Before evaluating any technology, map out your core business processes. Identify bottlenecks, repetitive tasks, and communication gaps. Technology should solve a real problem, not create a new one.

2. Set a Realistic Budget

ICT investment ranges widely. Define a budget that covers not just upfront costs but also training, maintenance, and future upgrades. Many cloud-based solutions offer flexible monthly pricing ideal for SMEs.

3. Consider Scalability

The solution you deploy today should grow with your business. Ask vendors how their product scales and what the cost implications are as your team or data grows.

4. Prioritise Security and Compliance

In an era of rising cyber threats, any ICT solution must meet security best practices and, where applicable, comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

5. Partner with a Local Expert

Working with a locally based ICT firm like Jasiri Tech Solutions means faster support, contextual understanding of the Kenyan business environment, and a team you can actually meet.
